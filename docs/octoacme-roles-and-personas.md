# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions
- Works with **Technical Leads** on architectural decisions and code quality
- Partners with **QA/Testing Leads** on test coverage and quality gates
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on acceptance criteria and requirements
- Coordinates with **DevOps/Release Engineers** on deployment processes

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions
- Works with **Stakeholder/Business Owners** on strategic priorities and business impact
- Collaborates with **Technical Leads** on feasibility and scope trade-offs
- Partners with **UX/Design** on user needs and usability requirements
- Aligns with **QA/Testing Leads** on quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Reports to **Project Managers** on scope changes and milestone impacts

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on scope, timeline, and deliverables
- Works with **Stakeholder/Business Owners** on priorities and approvals
- Coordinates with **Technical Leads** on technical risks and dependencies
- Partners with **DevOps/Release Engineers** on deployment scheduling and readiness
- Facilitates communication between all team members and stakeholders

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads define technical direction, ensure architectural soundness, and guide the development team on best practices. They balance innovation with stability and maintainability.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical direction and architecture for the project
- Conduct design reviews and provide technical guidance
- Identify and escalate technical risks and architectural debt
- Mentor and support developers on technical decisions
- Evaluate technology choices and propose solutions
- Ensure code quality, scalability, and maintainability standards

### Goals
- Build scalable, maintainable, and resilient systems
- Reduce technical debt and long-term costs
- Enable team growth through mentoring and knowledge sharing
- Prevent costly architectural rework

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code reviews and technical mentoring
- Risk escalation to Project Managers and Stakeholders
- Collaboration on CI/CD and infrastructure needs

### Interactions
- Guides **Developers** on implementation approach and technical decisions
- Advises **Product Managers** on technical feasibility and effort estimates
- Escalates technical blockers and risks to **Project Managers**
- Partners with **DevOps/Release Engineers** on infrastructure and deployment strategy
- Collaborates with **QA/Testing Leads** on testability and test strategy

---

## QA / Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads own quality strategy, coordinate testing efforts, and ensure products meet acceptance criteria and quality standards. They act as advocates for quality throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and quality standards
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Validate acceptance criteria and feature completeness
- Triage, track, and prioritize bugs
- Report quality metrics and trends
- Perform pre-release quality verification and smoke testing
- Identify quality risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early and reduce production incidents
- Maintain quality metrics and continuous improvement
- Enable confident releases

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Bug tracking and triage meetings
- Pre-release quality sign-offs
- Quality metrics and trend analysis

### Interactions
- Partners with **Developers** on test coverage, automation, and quality gates
- Works with **Product Managers** on acceptance criteria validation
- Collaborates with **Technical Leads** on testability and test strategy
- Coordinates with **DevOps/Release Engineers** on pre-release testing and smoke tests
- Reports quality status to **Project Managers** and **Stakeholders**

---

## DevOps / Release Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps/Release Engineers manage deployment pipelines, coordinate releases, maintain production health, and enable safe, reliable deployments. They are responsible for infrastructure and deployment automation.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Coordinate release planning and deployment scheduling
- Monitor production health, performance, and uptime
- Manage infrastructure, scaling, and disaster recovery
- Respond to incidents and coordinate rollbacks when needed
- Automate manual processes and improve deployment velocity
- Document runbooks and incident response procedures

### Goals
- Enable safe, fast, and reliable releases
- Maintain high production uptime and performance
- Reduce deployment risk and incident response time
- Improve deployment velocity and automation

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reports and deployment coordination
- Incident response and post-incident retrospectives
- Infrastructure planning and CI/CD improvements
- Operational metrics and monitoring dashboards

### Interactions
- Works with **Developers** on CI/CD integration and deployment best practices
- Coordinates with **QA/Testing Leads** on pre-release verification and smoke testing
- Aligns with **Technical Leads** on infrastructure and scalability needs
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on release timing and deployment windows
- Partners with **Stakeholders** on production readiness and deployment risks

---

## Stakeholder / Business Owner

### Role Summary
Stakeholders/Business Owners represent business interests, provide strategic direction, and approve scope changes and major decisions. They ensure the project aligns with organizational goals and priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic direction
- Approve scope changes and major feature decisions
- Prioritize work based on business impact and ROI
- Secure budget, resources, and organizational support
- Define success metrics aligned with business objectives
- Receive and act on project status updates
- Approve release timing and go/no-go decisions

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers business value and ROI
- Align delivery with organizational strategy
- Minimize business risk and disruption
- Maximize stakeholder satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and status briefings
- Budget and resource reviews
- Priority and scope change approvals
- Post-release impact analysis and metrics reviews

### Interactions
- Works with **Product Managers** on strategic priorities and roadmap alignment
- Collaborates with **Project Managers** on schedule, scope, and resource approvals
- Reviews release readiness with **DevOps/Release Engineers** and **Project Managers**
- Approves major decisions and trade-offs with input from **Technical Leads** and **QA/Testing Leads**
- Communicates organizational priorities to all team members

---

## UX / Design (If Applicable)

### Role Summary
UX/Design professionals define user experience, conduct research to understand user needs, and create design specifications. They ensure products are usable, accessible, and delightful.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and validate user needs
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Perform usability testing and gather user feedback
- Ensure accessibility and design system compliance
- Collaborate on design trade-offs and iterations
- Review implementation against design specifications
- Advocate for user needs throughout the project

### Goals
- Deliver products that are intuitive and enjoyable to use
- Reduce user friction and improve adoption
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design
- Reduce rework from poor UX decisions

### Typical Communication
- Design specs and component guidelines
- Usability test findings and recommendations
- Design review feedback and approval
- User research insights and opportunities

### Interactions
- Partners with **Developers** on design implementation and quality
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on user research and requirements
- Works with **QA/Testing Leads** on usability testing
- Advises **Stakeholders** on user impact of design decisions
- Participates in retrospectives to improve design process

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- Reference these personas when creating project charters, planning documents, and communication plans to ensure all necessary roles are identified and their responsibilities are clear.
